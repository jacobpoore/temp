About My Journey

I’m a mathematics student and ocean engineer developing tools and models for the marine environment. I explore how algorithms, design, and fluid dynamics come together to tackle subsea challenges. My passion lies in the intersection of the deep blue and innovative solutions.

Ocean Modeling – Simulation of wave and current systems using MATLAB and Python, bringing the ocean's dynamics to life through code.

Marine Tech Design – Hands-on engineering with subsea cables, robust structures, and embedded systems, crafting solutions for the harsh marine frontier.

Applied Math – Leveraging tools like differential equations and numerical analysis to drive innovation and solve complex problems in challenging aquatic environments.

Technical Skills

  • MATLAB for fluid dynamics & modeling
  • Python for automation and data visualization
  • Arduino & embedded systems for marine sensing
  • CAD/SolidWorks for subsea hardware design

Experience

Makai Ocean Engineering – Contributing to subsea cable modeling and offshore deployment plans, navigating the complexities of deep-sea infrastructure.

Wesleyan University – Pursuing a B.A. in Mathematics with applied coursework in differential equations, mechanics, and environmental modeling, building a strong theoretical foundation for practical applications.
